⋮ ⌗ ┆ Welcome to Learnix!

“Learn with Focus. Study with Purpose.”

Learnix is an online platform designed to help junior high school students learn web development in a simple and organized way. Our site provides step-by-step lessons and activities on key web design topics, so students can study at their own pace and build a strong foundation in coding and styling web pages. Our Mission is to make learning web development easy, understandable, and accessible for junior high students, helping them gain confidence and practical skills in creating websites. Our target audience are junior high school students who want to understand how websites work and how to style them with HTML and CSS. No prior experience is required — just curiosity and the willingness to learn!
